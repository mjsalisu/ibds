Home How It Works

Step 1: Register as a Donor or Student

  • Donors can easily create an account and start exploring donation options.
  • Students, verified by their institutions, can submit request to seek financial aid.

    • Step 2: Choose a Student to Support

  • Browse profiles of verified students and select the ones you wish to support. You can filter based on criteria such as gender, disability status, academic performance, and more.

    • Step 3: Make a Donation

  • Donate directly through our secure platform. All funds goes directly to the institution, ensuring they are used for the intended purpose.

    • Step 4: Track Your Impact

  • Receive real-time updates on your donations and see how your contributions are helping students achieve their educational goals.
